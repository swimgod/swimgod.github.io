**Definition:**

Control is the final step of the pipeline taking in the projected trajectory and turning it into actuator inputs that cause the AV to steer left or right and throttle or brake.

**What is an actuator?**

An actuator is a component that is responsible for the movement and control of a system. It takes in a control signal that is created from the path planning step and converts the signal into a mechanical action.
