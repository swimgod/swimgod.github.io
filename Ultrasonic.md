**Definition:**

Ultrasonic sensors use high frequency sound waves to detect nearby objects at short to medium range distances at low speed.

**How an Ultrasonic Sensor Works:**

An ultrasonic sensor imitates the navigation process of bats by sending out sound waves. When the waves hit an object, it produces echoes that reveal the location of the obstacle.

**Characteristics of Ultrasonic sensors:**

**1. Useful for automated parking:** Ultrasonic sensors are useful for detecting obstacles at close distances.

**2. Cost Effective:** Ultrasonic sensors are cheaper than cameras, despite their limited functionality.

**3. Not crucial for autonomous driving:** Ultrasonic sensors are not a key sensor used in ADAS systems. Very little is being invested into the technology, and it has its defined purpose in low speed applications.
